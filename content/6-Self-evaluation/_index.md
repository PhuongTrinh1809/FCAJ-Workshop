Throughout **12 weeks** of internship in the **First Cloud AI Journey** program, I continuously learned and practiced on AWS — starting from foundational concepts, then gradually moving to architecture design, deployment, and operation of the **ZeroBug Agent** system in a real cloud environment.

In my role developing **ZeroBug Agent** — an AI platform that automatically generates Unit Tests for software projects — I worked with multiple AWS services including Cognito, API Gateway, EC2, RDS, Bedrock, Lambda, Step Functions, S3, CloudFront, WAF, and CloudWatch. This process helped me gain further experience in **cloud architecture design, application security, CI/CD deployment, and applying AI to products**.

During implementation, I tried to follow the weekly plan, proactively looked up and resolved technical issues as they arose, and referred to official AWS documentation while sharing and learning from the community.
